Hello!

My name is Anton Konev. I develop apps & launch online-services. I help companies build fast and user-friendly interfaces and reach their goals.

I prefer to make projects from scratch: to build architecture, to prototype, to collect metrics and work fast. Interested in challenging projects in ML.

At present, I’m a Technical Lead at ARRIVAL Ltd.

I launched Youtube channel «Anton code it» with Javascript live coding sessions. I share knowledge about JS, discuss my solutions and solutions of my colleagues.

Before that, I was а team lead & frontend developer at Yandex. As a team, we built an expandable and easy-to-support platform on which we developed and launched 3 large internal services in a short time. Prior to this, I implemented a new version of Yandex.Browser, participated in the development of Yandex.Weather and a special project for the Olympics in Rio.

I was a member of the Program Committee of YaTalks conference — hundreds of participants, frontend & backend track, 16 speakers and lots of mentions and positive feedback.

I taught a frontend course at the Ural Federal University. In 2018 I’ve become a co-author of frontend courses on Coursera.
